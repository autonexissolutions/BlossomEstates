Our Journey

2005 - Foundation

SCC Group was established with a vision to create quality housing solutions in the rapidly growing NCR region.

2010 - First Major Project

Successfully delivered our first residential complex in Ghaziabad, setting new standards in the local market.

2005 - ISO Certification

Achieved ISO certification, formalizing our commitment to quality management systems and processes.

2018 - Commercial Ventures

Expanded into commercial real estate with SCC Biz Plaza, our first premium office space project.

2023 - Current Projects

Our Associations

Proud Member of CREDAI

SCC Group is a proud member of CREDAI (Confederation of Real Estate Developers' Associations of India), the apex body for private real estate developers in India. This membership reflects our commitment to ethical business practices and adherence to the highest standards in the real estate industry.

CREDAI represents over 12,000 developers across 23 states and 221 city chapters in India.
